Pomodoro Timer

The rules of the Pomodoro timer are very simple. You work for 25 minutes, then you have a 5min break after 4 cycles you have the right to a 15min break. Take the opportunity to make yourself a coffee and walk around a bit.

25 : 00

Short break: 5:00

Long break : 15:00
